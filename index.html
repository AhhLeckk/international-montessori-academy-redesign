✨ Our Mission

Built on Love, Harmony & Beauty

Our mission is to provide an atmosphere of learning that is LOVING emotionally, and physically and spiritually SAFE — one that creates a living and unique Montessori environment where children learn through "doing."

We instill tolerance, thoughtfulness, consideration, and responsibility. We celebrate differences in others. IMA values diversity and seeks families of all racial, cultural, religious, and economic backgrounds.

❤️
Love
Children are born with the natural tendency to love — the platform from which they learn most effectively.
🕊️
Harmony
When there is understanding and knowledge, harmony is reached. We rise above differences together.
🌸
Beauty
We inspire children to have ideals, appreciate beauty, and find joy and wonder in learning.
🧠
Follow the Child
We create independent learners who attain their personal best at their own level, speed, and rhythm.

📐 Academic Excellence

Montessori academics rooted in California standards

Every classroom blends the self-directed Montessori method with rigorous, grade-level academics aligned to California's curriculum frameworks — so students build deep understanding while staying on track for whatever school comes next.

📖
English Language Arts
Montessori language materials build phonemic awareness, reading fluency, and writing skills in step with California ELA standards.
🔢
Mathematics
Hands-on materials like the golden beads and bead chains build true number sense while covering California math progressions K–6.
🔬
Science
The Cosmic Curriculum's hands-on experiments and nature study align with Next Generation Science Standards used across California.
🌎
History & Social Science
Timeline work and cultural studies build the geography, civics, and history foundations outlined in California's Social Science framework.

Note: Curriculum is designed to align with California academic content standards while preserving the independence and pacing of the Montessori method. Contact your campus director for detailed scope and sequence by grade.

❓ Common Questions

Things families ask us

What are your student-to-teacher ratios?+
Our small class sizes ensure every student receives individualized attention throughout their day — a hallmark of the Montessori approach.
What is the Montessori method?+
Developed by Dr. Maria Montessori — a scientist, mathematician, and physician — the Montessori method is based on the premise that each child is born with a drive to develop themselves. Children learn best through physical activity and the senses, given freedom to discover, explore, and create in an enriched environment.
What are Montessori materials and why are they different?+
Montessori materials are self-directed and self-correcting — they activate a desire to learn more because they engage children both physically and mentally. Each material develops one perception at a time, placing responsibility for finding answers on the child and building independence and self-motivation.
Who are your teachers?+
All lead teachers hold Montessori credentials and are highly qualified, experienced, innovative, and creative. Our teachers emphasize moral instruction, tolerance, and kindness, supported by ECE-qualified assistants at every campus.
Do you accept families of different backgrounds and faiths?+
Absolutely. International Montessori Academy values diversity and actively seeks families of all racial, cultural, religious, and economic backgrounds. Our founding philosophy of Love, Harmony, and Beauty was built on celebrating differences — not minimizing them.
What extracurricular activities are available?+
Extracurricular activities vary by campus and season. Please contact your campus director to learn which activities and enrichment programs are currently being offered.
How do I find tuition and fees?+
Tuition varies by campus and program level. Please visit our Tuition & Fees page or contact your preferred campus directly. We encourage all interested families to reach out regardless of financial situation.
